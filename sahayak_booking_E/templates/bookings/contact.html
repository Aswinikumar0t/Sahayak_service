Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I book a service? +
Booking a service is easy! Simply visit our Services page, select the service you need, choose your preferred date and time, fill in your details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation message immediately.
Are the professionals verified? +
Yes! All our service professionals undergo a thorough verification process including background checks, ID verification, and skill assessment before they can join our platform. Your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities.
What is your cancellation policy? +
You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Cancellations made within 2 hours of the service may incur a small cancellation fee. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
How can I become a service partner? +
Visit our "Become a Partner" page and fill out the application form. Our team will review your application, verify your credentials, and contact you within 5-7 business days to guide you through the onboarding process.
