Our Story

How Sahayak started its journey

Sahayak was born from a simple observation - finding reliable home service professionals in India is often frustrating and time-consuming. People struggle to find trusted electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and other service providers when they need them the most.

In 2024, a group of passionate individuals came together with a vision to transform the home service industry. We created Sahayak to bridge the gap between customers and skilled professionals, ensuring quality, transparency, and reliability in every interaction.

Today, Sahayak has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of satisfied customers across India. We continue to innovate and improve our services, staying true to our commitment to making home maintenance effortless and reliable.
